Greekin' Out Charleston

OUR INSPIRATION

Greekin' Out Food Truck was inspired due to the desire of a family; mother and daughter and son, wanting to be together. They chose traditional Greek cuisine where partner Dorian Arsi could utilize his delicious, made from scratch, authentic family recipes.

OUR FOOD TRAILER

Charlestons new and unique food trailer serves traditional Greek food, Gyros, homemade Tzatziki and sauces, fresh cooked marinated meats, flatbreads, zesty orzo bowls, amazing dressed fries, Vegetarian options and more.
