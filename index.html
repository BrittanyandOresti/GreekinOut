Greekin' Out Charleston

Behind Greekin' Out

Greekin' Out Food Truck was started as most great food is, family tradition. Casey Parish with her mom Vicki, brother Michael and partner Dorian Arsi, keep traditional Greek street food alive thanks to the Arsi family recipes and Southern charms of the family.

Welcome to Charleston

Charlestons newest and hottest food truck serves up traditional Greek Gyros with a famous handmade Tzatziki and fresh sliced gyros. Vegetarian options include a zesty orzo bowl, vegetable pita, and amazing dressed fries.
